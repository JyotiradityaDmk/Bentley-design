Performance

Created for effortless, exhilarating driving, the new Continental GT is buolt on an innovative chassis and electrical architecture, featuring a completely new 6.0 litre W12 engine and an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission, for smooth, fast and efficient gear changes. As a result, it can accelarate from 0 to 60 mph in 3.6 seconds (0 to 100 km/h in 3.7 seconds) and deliver a phenomenal top speed of 207 mph (333 km/h)

Four driving modes are accessible at the turn of a dial. Comfort optimises the chasis and drivetrain for luxurious cruising, while Sport mode returns the engine for a more dynamic response- with the variable exhaust note to match. The Bentley mode provides the optimum combination of Sport and Comfort, while a Custom mode allows you to configure your own settings.
